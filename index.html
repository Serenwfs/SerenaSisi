Bio

Serena is an independent artist whose music is as eclectic as her background. Originally from France, her life's journey through five different countries, including Africa and the United States, has deeply influenced her sound. With over 13 years of experience, Serena's music seamlessly blends alternative R&B and pop with the diverse rhythms of her travels. Her passion for music started in her childhood, surrounded by the melodies of her guitar-playing father and the rich tapestry of Cameroonian, African, and French variety music. Serena's songs are a reflection of her life experiences, exploring themes of love, mental health, and the joys and complexities of human emotion. Each track she writes is an intimate glimpse into her world, inviting listeners to connect and find a piece of themselves in her music. As much as her music is about storytelling, it's also about connecting. Serena believes in the power of music to transcend barriers and bring people together. With a love for the unconventional, her artistry is a blend of the familiar and the extraordinary. Join Serena on her musical journey, where every note tells a story. -Love Alien
